Dream Financial Services Limited Company is a new Micro Finance Institution. It offers a broad range of financial services to micro, small and medium-sized enterprises, and various low-income earners as individuals, businesses, and groups like cooperatives.

Dream Financial Services Limited Company is fully committed to provide various saving deposits for all levels of customers. It is a limited liability company duly incorporated in Malawi under the 2017 Companies Act and is regulated by the Reserve Bank of Malawi

Dream Financial Services.

To provide entrepreneurs with unique, people-oriented, technology-driven, value-added products and services delivered by a well-trained and motivated workforce in a sustainable and profitable manner for the economic benefit of our stakeholders.

Our Mission.

Encourage among the un banked productive citizens of the country. To make credit facilities more accessible to micro-entrepreneurs engaged in real sector activities at an attractive interest rate and in a sustainable manner. Improve the business and financial skills of micro-entrepreneurs through capacity building and training.

FIXED TERM DEPOSIT
PRODUCT TARGET
For customers with excess funds who intend to commit to an investment for a fixed period of time.
PRODUCT FEATURES
  • Minimum Balance of MWK50,000.
  • Predetermined fixed period
  • Interest is paid on expiry of each contractual period.
BENEFITS TO CUSTOMERS
  • No bank charges.

  • Very low risk.
ACCOUNT OPENING REQUIREMENTS
  • Account opening forms

STUDENT ACCOUNT
PRODUCT TARGET
For students aged least 18 years.
PRODUCT FEATURES
  • Minimum Balance of MWK1,000.
  • Interest is at 4% per annul. Subject to review
BENEFITS TO CUSTOMERS
  • No service fees on personal accounts.
ACCOUNT OPENING REQUIREMENTS

LOAN SERVICES
We get you covered
Working Capital Loan.
A working capital loan is designed for micro-entrepreneurs and small business owners who desire to borrow for business purposes to enhance their businesses.
Loan Size
MK250,000 to MK5, 000,000
Interest Rate
4% Monthly (using the reducing balance method)
Requirements
Must be in business for at least 6 Months
Asset Finance Loan
Asset finance is a credit product that gives you the opportunity to own that asset you have always longed for either for Business or for Personal use.
Asset Cost
Not exceeding MK35,000,000
Interest Rate
4% Monthly (reducing balance)
Maximum Repayment Period
36 months
Consumer Loan/Pay Roll Loans
This is designed for workers who want to use the facility for personal, family, or household purposes, or for consumable items like household appliances and payment of school fees.
Loan Size
MK250,000 - MK2,000,000
Loan Interest
4%
Requirements
Must be working in a reputable organization or company (Salary Earner)
Group Loans
Group loans are designed for micro-entrepreneurs, market women, self-help groups, and associations that are active in the business.
Group Loan Amount
From MK 100,000 to MK 500,000
Group Member Exposure
Not more than 30% of the total group loan
Interest Rate
5% Monthly (reducing balance)
